Official Objectives

The BDS Wing of the Shivsena Doctors Cell stands as the premier structural body dedicated to protecting the rights, resolving localized operational concerns, and elevating the academic standard of dental undergraduate students across Maharashtra. Serving under a unified state vision, we systematically bridge communication loops between healthcare institutional heads, MUHS university systems, and the student body.

Konkan Division Administrative Hub

Monitoring and regulating student affairs across all private, semi-aided, and government municipal corporations dental colleges within the geographical jurisdiction of the Konkan division.

Institutional Representation

Establishing local structural student grievance desks inside every dental campus across the Konkan belt.

Stipend & Infrastructure Tracking

Ensuring systematic execution of operational mandates and fair payout patterns for interns.

Statewide Student Grievance Desk

BDS Undergraduate students, interns, or resident doctors facing infrastructural delays, university standard anomalies, or unethical institutional mandates anywhere across Maharashtra can securely report directly to the cell core hierarchy.

Processing Timeline

Submitted issues are verified by regional committee panels within 48 business hours and routed to state leadership desks for systemic administrative execution.
