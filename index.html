Empowering Dental Students Across Maharashtra

The BDS Wing of the Shivsena Doctors Cell is dedicated to the welfare, rights, and academic excellence of dental undergraduate students, interns, and young professionals across the state. We serve as the bridge between students and administrative bodies to ensure a progressive and supportive environment for the future of dentistry.

Our Focus Areas

  • Student Grievance Redressal: Active support for issues related to university exams, stipends, and campus infrastructure.
  • Academic Growth: Organizing CDE programs, clinical hands-on workshops, and career guidance seminars.
  • Community Health: Conducting statewide dental health checkup camps and rural awareness drives.
